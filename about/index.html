About Us

- OUR VISION -

One World Academy is an educational institution which strives to develop to the fullest the potential of each of its members in a school environment based on respect, academic excellence and responsibility.
The School aims to provide an internationally competitive bilingual education with a highly dedicated, motivated and committed teaching and managerial staff.
One World Academy promotes the well-rounded development of all of its students and emphasizes the importance of leading a well-balanced life. Morality, ethics, honesty, respect and accountability are highly cherished.

- OUR MISSION -

One World Academy:
  • Provides a well-rounded education for every student within a broad and inclusive curriculum (academic, social, civil, cultural and sporting).
  • Promotes a stimulating and challenging learning environment founded on tolerance, mutual respect, caring relationships, moral accountability, personal integrity and professional commitment.
  • Recognizes the need to work closely and effectively with all stakeholders.
